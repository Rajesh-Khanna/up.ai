AI for Social Good

About the hackathon

Our world faces numerous challenges like diseases, natural disasters, illiteracy, low-crop yields etc. We are hopeful that Artificial Intelligence can provide an answer to many of these problems and raise the status quo of the society. Deep Learning has shown massive potential to extract useful patterns from massive amount of data, patterns that can be immensely useful for our society. So come join us and help us build an AI powered India.

up.AI Workshop
up.AI Challenge

A two day workshop tailored to acquaint the participants with relevant state of the art techniques in the field of artificial intelligence that would help them put their ideas to practice for the up.AI Challenge.

The participants are invited to form teams of size three and submit project proposals in the aforesaid domains. 10 projects are selected for incubation during the winter vacation under the mentorship of interested professors, PhD students and industry-experts to convert the ideas into deployable solutions.
